# Page Schemas

## Wiki Pages

```yaml
title: <string>
created: YYYY-MM-DD
updated: YYYY-MM-DD
type: entity | concept | comparison | query | summary
tags: <list>
sources: <list>
confidence: high | medium | low
contested: <bool>
contradictions: <list>
```

### Tag Taxonomy
- **Domains:** identity, strategic-thinking, systems-design, psychology, cognitive, autonomy, execution, business, technology, philosophy, narrative, leadership
- **Qualities:** core-strength, core-weakness, operational-pattern, psychological-trap, non-negotiable, healing-path, synthesis
- **Meta:** comparison, timeline, contradiction-flag, foundational

## Post Frontmatter

```yaml
title: <string>
platform: x | linkedin | blog | buffer
status: draft | queued | ready-to-publish | published | rejected | held
created: YYYY-MM-DD
type: pillar-system | pillar-story | pillar-teardown | mini
archetype: S1-S10
vertical: <string>
funnel_stage: TOFU | MOFU | BOFU
icp_layer: L1 | L2 | L3 | L4
core_insight: <string>
meaning_axis: systemic | behavioral | philosophical | contrarian | leverage | human
banner: <path>
session_id: <string>
tags: <list>
standalone_test: passed | almost | failed | skipped
```

## Session Log

```yaml
title: <string>
date: YYYY-MM-DD
type: session
sources: <list>
tags: <list>
archetype: S1-S10
vertical: <string>
funnel_stage: TOFU | MOFU | BOFU
icp_layer: L1 | L2 | L3 | L4
reader_failure_mode:
  belief: <string>
  consequence: <string>
  mapping: <string>
```

## Banner Frontmatter

Every queue draft must have a `banner:` field:

```yaml
banner: assets/banners/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.png
```

Generated by `banner_tool.py` during the BANNER state. Stored in `assets/banners/`.
