SpielOS

A markdown-driven marketing team for your content pipeline.

SpielOS is a marketing team that lives inside your IDE.

You bring the work. The team — MD, Strategist, Researcher, Copywriter, Editor, Designer, Publisher, Analyst — turns it into posts for X, LinkedIn, and your blog. You stay a builder.

The wizard sets up the 8 roles and the 8 knowledge files the system needs to write posts in your voice, for your audience, about your work.

10 steps. About 5 minutes. You can edit everything later.
