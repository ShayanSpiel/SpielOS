# Blog Post Template (Pillar)

The blog post is the *source of truth* — X and LinkedIn posts are *sampled* from it.
Copy to `content/queue/YYYY-MM-DD-pillar-blog.md` and fill in.

## Body

```markdown
# <H1>

## Intro (3-5 sentences, the hook)

<state the problem in 2-3 sentences. do not preamble. no "in this article, I will...".>

## <H2 Section 1>

<1 idea. 200-300 words.>

## <H2 Section 2>

<1 idea. 200-300 words.>

## <H2 Section 3>

<1 idea. 200-300 words.>

## <H2 Section 4 (optional)>

<1 idea. 200-300 words.>

## <H2 Section 5 — Counter-arguments (optional)>

<the strongest counter to the post's claim. address it. if you cannot, the claim is wrong.>

## <H2 Section 6 — Try it (CTA)>

<what the reader should do in the next 24 hours. one paragraph.>

## Conclusion (1 paragraph)

<the one-line takeaway. the landing.>
```

## Atomization plan (fill in before posting)

After writing the pillar, sample from it:

- [ ] 1-3 LinkedIn posts (use [[linkedin-post]])
  - [ ] LI #1 samples: <section name>
  - [ ] LI #2 samples: <section name>
  - [ ] LI #3 samples: <section name>
- [ ] 1-10 X posts (use [[x-post]])
  - [ ] X #1 samples: <the insight>
  - [ ] X #2 samples: <the insight>
  - [ ] X #3 samples: <the insight>
- [ ] Optional thread (7-12 tweets)

Every atomized post must pass the 4-check + 10-gate.

## Check before saving

- [ ] Hook passes 5-second test
- [ ] Each section carries one idea
- [ ] Counter-arguments addressed or not needed
- [ ] Atomization plan filled in
- [ ] Frontmatter complete
