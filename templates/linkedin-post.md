# LinkedIn Post Template

Copy to `content/queue/YYYY-MM-DD-linkedin-NN.md` and fill in. The post must
pass the 4-check baseline + the 10-gate extended (see `strategy/voice.md`).

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: <one-line — names pain or payoff, NOT project name>
banner: <auto-generated by pipeline, e.g. assets/banners/YYYY-MM-DD-title.png>
platform: linkedin
type: <story|perception-shift|metaphor|opening-statement|casual-update|case-study>
pattern: <counter-intuitive|specific-number|cheat-code|confessional|delete-reframe|list-promise|none>
pillar: <path to the source pillar blog post, or "none">
sampled_from: <section of the pillar this post samples>
voice_register: <casual|polished>
icp: this post helps a <reader> do <thing> in <time>
engagement_ask: <one from rules.yaml §engagement_bank>
status: draft
created: YYYY-MM-DD
standalone_test: <passed|almost|failed|skipped>
copywriting_gate: <passed|almost|failed>
tags: [<angle>, <angle2>]
---
```

## Body

```markdown
## Hook (2 lines, must pass 5-sec test in feed)

<line 1 — counter-intuitive, specific number, named pain, or aha>
<line 2 — the tension, the contradiction, the surprise, or "here's what most miss">

[LINE BREAK — critical for mobile readability]

## Setup

<1-2 short paragraphs. every sentence on its own line. blank line between paragraphs.
line 3 of the post (after the hook) is the promise — what the reader gets if they keep reading.>

## Core

<1-3 paragraphs that carry the argument, the story, or the question.
800-1500 chars for casual / 1300-3000 chars for polished.
no noun 3+ times. no "the engine" word loop.>

## Lesson (last 1-2 lines)

<the lesson is the *last* line, not the first.>

## Ask (last line)

<one of the engagement_bank phrases from rules.yaml — NOT "I be happy" if used in last 7 days.>
```
