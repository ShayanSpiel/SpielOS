# /setup — Configure Engine

12-question questionnaire in 4 blocks. Writes answers into the engine config.

## Blocks

### 1. Identity (3 questions)
1. What is your brand name and tagline?
2. Who are you as a creator? (1-2 sentences)
3. What is the core idea your content revolves around?

### 2. ICP (3 questions)
4. Who is your ideal reader? (demographics, psychographics)
5. What problem does your ICP struggle with daily? (4 layers)
6. What 7 questions does your ICP ask internally?

### 3. Offer (3 questions)
7. What do you sell in the customer's words?
8. How does the ICP describe the problem before your solution?
9. What do they say after?

### 4. Voice (3 questions)
10. Describe your writing voice in 3 words.
11. Paste your 3 best posts. (The LLM will analyze for patterns.)
12. What is one thing you never want to sound like?

Each block has a "Skip — I'll edit the file myself" option.

## What gets written

- concepts/icp-offer.md (ICP + offer sections)
- concepts/funnel-and-matrix.md (verticals, archetypes)
- concepts/voice-corpus.md (canonical examples)
- rules.yaml (strategy section)
- assets/brand-config.json

The questionnaire can be re-run to update any section.
